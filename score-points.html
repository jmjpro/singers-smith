How to Score Points

Each word formed contributes to the total score by multiplying the sum of the values of each letter in the word by the number of letters in the word. For example, the word "dive" has a value of 36. (3 + 1 + 4 + 1) * 4

Consider the following sequence of plays:

  1. Player forms the word "car" in row #3. He receives (3 + 1 + 3) * 3 = 21 points for the word "car"
  2. Later in the game he turns the word "car" into "trace". He now receives (3 + 3 + 1 + 3 + 1) * 5 = 55 points for the word "trace."
  3. Later in the game he turns the word "trace" into "caters". He receives (3 + 1 + 3 + 1 + 3 + 3) * 6 = 84 points for the word "caters"

At this point the player has accumulated 160 points for his entries into row #3. Now the word "caters" disappears since it is a six letter word and the player can begin a new word in row #3 and start accumulating more points.
