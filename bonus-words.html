Bonus Words

When the player starts a new game, a screen will appear which will allow the player to choose one of three themes: silversmith, locksmith, or no theme. When the player presses the icon for the theme that he chooses another screen will appear with the bonus words for that theme. An icon on that screen will say "Start Game." The player will press this icon when he has finished looking at the bonus words and would like to start playing.

Bonus words will award player with additional points in the following manner:

  • 3-letter bonus word: 100 additional points
  • 4-letter bonus word: 200 additional points
  • 5- letter bonus word: 300 additional points
  • 6-letter bonus word: 400 additional points
